MONO ACTING

Rules General Rules


RULES

1. Time Limit: 10 minutes + 5 mins including setup.
2. Number of participants: 3 – 10 members
3. Open teams are allowed.
4. Only one person per team must register.
5. Two levels of screening
6. For first level screening, the link for the videos must be submitted at the time of online registration (Videos of past performances are accepted.)
7. Shortlisted bands from this first level will be intimated through mail.
8. Only shortlisted bands will receive the payment link through email.
9. Payment must be completed online via the link before the deadline published.
10. Participants should bring their own instruments.
11. The songs to be performed on stage must be only in Indian languages.
12. A standard 5 - piece drum kit with one bass amplifier and guitar amplifier will be provided.
13. Judges’ decision will always be final.

VENUE: Outdoor Stage

GENERAL RULES

1. Participants must bring their photo IDs.
2. Participants without photo ID cards will not be allowed to participate.
3. Verification will be done at the registration desk.
4. Students can participate in more than one event.
5. All the participants have to register online.
6. Registration fees must be paid online for all the events.
7. Last date for online registrations is March 7th.
8. Accommodation, if required must be intimated during registration.
9. Participants are requested to report to the venue half an hour before the event.
10. Students should strictly abide by the rules and regulations of the event.
