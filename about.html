Rajalakshmi Institutions is one of the most prestigious institutions in the state. From securing university level ranks to maing our mark in National level events, the students have always been over achieving.

We believe that a person's growth is complete only if he/she gets to fulfill his/her passion. At our instituition this is strictly followed. The students are always encouraged by the institution to excel in their passion be it dance, music, drama or debate.

We have a fully functional music band called UNISON that has been the winner of several accolades around the state.

A regular participant and winners of several intercollege culturals events, the students of Rajalakshmi Institutions have set their mark everywhere.
In 2018, we hosted our first ever intra college cultural fest, RECKLESS. The overwhelming response has pushed us to take this to another step and up our game.

This year, as another gem to is all set to host its first ever Inter-collegiate Cultural Fest - RECharge'19. RECharge'19 will be a one of a kind cultural fest. We have a range of events that covers Dance, Drama, Music and others with attractive prizes for top performers in each event. Our events focus on individual and group performances and we will have the best of judges mentoring the participants. We feel that this event will be a great boost to not only our institutions but to our participants as well. We hope to provide them a great experience that they will cherish for a long time.
