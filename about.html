MAR 11th,2019



Rajalakshmi Institutions is one of the premier Educational Institutions in the state. The institution provides various streams of study from Architecture, Engineering, Management and Nursing. From securing university level ranks to making our mark I'm National events the students of this Institution have always achieved academically. At Rajalakshmi Institutions, we believe that a person’s growth is complete only if he/she gets to fulfill their passion. Our institution has strictly followed this belief. Our motto has always been "To provide technically competent professionals with character and humane values."

The students’ are always encouraged by the institution to excel not only in academics but also in their passion be it dance, music, drama or debate. To aid the overall development the Institution has pioneered in implementing scientifically designed value addition programmes. The experience that the students gain at this institution extents outside their classrooms. They are encouraged to positively influence the society they live in. This is done through the various societies for music (UNISON) , literature ( Atrium and Debating Union), social services (NSS) , Rotaract and several other student in free enterprise fourims that the institution hosts.

All this make the students belonging to this institution a great addition to any organisation they are a part of. We have a fully functional music band called UNISON, who has been proud winners of several accolades around the state. Several practice sessions and jam sessions are conducted by the band and they are a delight to hear and watch. In the year 2018, we hosted our first ever intra college cultural fest called RECKLESS . The amazing response has pushed us to take a step forward.

This year as a gem to their crown the Institution is all set to host its first ever Inter-collegiate Cultural Fest RECHARGE'19 . RECHARGE'19 will be Rajalakshmi Institutions venture into the culturals zone. Through this we hope to build the students interests in various fields by providing them a great platform to express their talents and thereby encouraging them to give through to their passion. We have an array of events that cover the streams of Dance, Drama and Music and others. Our events focus on individual and group performances.
