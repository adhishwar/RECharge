INSTRUMENTAL – INTERLUDE

Interlude


RULES

1. Time Limit: 5 minutes.
2. Participants should bring their own instruments.
3. Participants are required to set up instruments without causing delays.
4. Two levels of screening.
5. For first level screening, the link for the videos must be submitted at the time of online registration (Videos of past performances are accepted.)
6. Shortlisted participants from this first level will be intimated through mail.
7. Only shortlisted individual will receive the payment link through email.
8. Payment must be completed online via the link before the deadline published.
9. A 5 Piece Drum kit will be provided.
10. Judges’ decision will always be final.
